About VaultETH

VaultETH is a decentralized application (dApp) that empowers users to set, track, and manage ETH-based savings goals using blockchain technology.

Whether you're saving up for a laptop, a vacation, or building an emergency fund, VaultETH lets you create personalized goals, contribute funds, track your progress in real time, and even leave motivational notes.

🔧 Features

  • 🎯 Create and track personalized ETH savings goals
  • 💸 Contribute to your goals from your wallet
  • 📈 View real-time progress with ETH → USD conversion
  • 📝 Leave notes or journal entries per campaign
  • 🎁 Mint Vault Tokens when goals are achieved

💡 Tech Stack

  • 📦 Solidity – Smart contracts (SavingsVault, VaultToken, Notes)
  • 📘 Remix IDE – Contract development
  • 🛠️ Ethers.js – Web3 integration
  • 🧠 Express.js – Backend routing
  • 🎨 Bootstrap – UI design and layout
  • 🦊 MetaMask + Sepolia – Wallet and testnet
